# Loopup — Product Context

## Product Purpose
Marketing landing page for Loopup, a mobile game app. The app ships a library of small games (solo and versus) and uses an AI generator to create personalized games based on how you play. One-time $4 unlock for the generator; no subscription, no ads.

## Register
brand

## Users
Casual mobile gamers aged 18–35 who are bored of predatory F2P game mechanics. People who play games with friends over text. People who want something to open at a bus stop and close when the bus comes.

## Brand Tone
Dry, confident, a little weird. Anti-hype. Describes things precisely rather than excitedly. The copy never oversells — it undersells and lets the product speak.

## Color Strategy
Drenched — pink-to-purple gradient is the identity. Lavender backgrounds, fuchsia/magenta/violet accents, dark purple footer.

## Typography
Instrument Serif — editorial, slightly unexpected for a game app. Large display sizes (56–82px) with tight letter-spacing.

## Anti-references
- No "Revolutionize / Transform / Unlock" language
- No glowing neon orbs as primary decoration
- No dark-with-neon as default scheme
- No gradient text
- No SaaS hero template (centered headline + two buttons)

## Strategic Principles
- Four sections: Overview, Mini Games, How It Works, Download
- Wipe transition between sections (no scroll)
- Three.js 3D elements: die, conveyor, phone
- Animated backgrounds per section (blob canvas, diamond grid, diagonal sweep, noise purple)
- Single-page app using React via CDN + Babel standalone
